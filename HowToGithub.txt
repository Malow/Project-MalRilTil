You want to update and get the latest version:
Right click on github folder, press pull. Copy all the files from the source-folder in github-folder to the source-folder where you are working. And same same with MyScripts.

You want to upload your changes:
Copy all files you've changed into this folder at the correct place from your working folder (source-files and script files).
Right click on folder -> Choose commit. Do not push!
Right click on folder, choose Pull! It should now properly merge the online-version with your version.
Now you right click on folder and choose push! And it will upload properly merged on the homepage.
